This course is intended for college-bound students. In this class, we will study angles,
degree and radian measurements, the six different trigonometric functions, graphing,
identities, solving equations, Law of Sines, Law of Cosines, and other trig. Related
applications.
Chapters to be covered
1. The trigonometric functions
2. Acute angles and right triangles
3. Radian measure and circular functions
4. Graphs of circular functions
5. Trigonometric identities
7. Applications of trigonometry and vectors
6. Inverse trigonometric functions and trigonometric equations?
